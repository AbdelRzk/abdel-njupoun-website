About Me

I am a researcher at Mila and pursuing my PhD at the University of Montreal, Quebec, Canada, where my research focuses on the intersection of computational neuroscience and artificial intelligence. My passion lies in leveraging mathematical principles to address and solve real-world challenges. I hold a Master of Engineering in Smart Systems from the National Higher Polytechnic School of Douala, Cameroon, a Master of Science in Mathematics from the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences Cameroon, and a Master of Science in Computer Science from the University of the Witwatersrand, South Africa.
